How we do it

The Catalyst Network is a fully functional decentralised technology stack that works in the real world.

Previous attempts to decentralise the internet have failed because they were too slow, resource intensive, specialised, insecure and poorly incentivised - there was little incentive for anyone to use them.

Catalyst Network has been built from the ground up to make the decentralised internet possible
- it’s light, fast, accessible, works with common programming languages, and is compatible within the current internet ecosystem. Most importantly, unlike mining-based networks, Catalyst rewards all its participants fairly through collaborative compensation.

Features

Fast

New ways of achieving consensus and managing data make Catalyst competitive with centralised servers.

Accessible

Catalyst works with the most common programming languages, not specialised code.

Democratic

Collaborative rather than competitive consensus gives all participants a fair stake.

Integrated

The Catalyst Network functions within the current internet ecosystem, with native integration of Docker and Kubernetes.

Light

Catalyst nodes require very little compute to function, and can run on IoT devices and Raspberry Pis.

Secure

The Catalyst DLT delivers the security of blockchain without the slow transaction times.

Catalyst Network

DAY ZERO NETWORK

Previous attempts to realise a decentralised internet have failed to reach viability, disappointing investors. Catalyst breaks that mould by offering a complete, functional Day Zero Network. By using virtualised nodes as a service, Catalyst will go live with a sustainable network in place - a world first.

GUARANTEED UTILITY

Investing in Catalyst is not investing in a promise - it’s securing a piece of a new reality. From Day Zero, there will be a viable network with functioning dApps.

PRIVATELY BUILT

The Catalyst Network is being developed by a seasoned team of tech and business veterans to provide the backbone of a fast and commercially viable decentralised internet and deployed by Atlas City to support their dApp ecosystem.

The Atlas City seed nodes are virtualised in the cloud and data centres distributed geographically to ensure network viability. Additional nodes will be bundled with the KAT tokens and accessible in the presale period for deployment on their infrastructure. Following the TGE and a predetermined test period, Atlas City will establish the Swiss Catalyst Foundation and transfer ownership of the network to its governance.
